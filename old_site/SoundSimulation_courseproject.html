Crowd Sound Simulation

Group Member

Aniket Bera, Shan Yang

Motivation

Sound effects in virtual reality creates the sense of realism and immersion. Crowd is an indispensable element of a virtual environment. The sound of the crowd plays a vital role when creating the experience of walking in the crowd.  In this project we wish to investigate the sound created by a crowd of people.

Challenge

The main challenge of this project is that there are a number of moving sound sources and moving obstacles.

Goal

The goal of this project is to combine crowd simulation with sound simulation. We aim to study the sound effects created by a moving crowd.
